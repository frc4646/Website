About


Wildcard Robotics 4646 is a robotics team based in Polk City, Iowa.
Wildcard consists of students from North Polk High School, Ankeny Centennial High School, Ankeny High School, and Madrid High School.
Wildcard started in 2021, taking over Team ASAP. View our history here
Wildcard meets at least once a week in Polk City. To join Wildcard, contact one of our adult volunteers.

Wildcard Robotics is a 4-H (Head, Heart, Hands, and Health) Robotics team. Visit the Polk County 4-H page here
