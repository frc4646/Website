Bean

♠ ♡ ♦ ♤ ♣ ♠ ♡ ♦ ♤ ♣

Originally a stray on a mentor’s property, Black Bean quickly claimed Team 4646 as her own. These days, she can usually be found exploring the workshop ceiling, playing with wires and other miscellaneous items, or enjoying conveniently unattended food.
At this point, it’s safe to say the team works for her—everything we do is in her honor, and just about everything ends up named after her.
