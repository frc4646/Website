Bean

♠ ♡ ♦ ♤ ♣ ♠ ♡ ♦ ♤ ♣

Originally a stray on a mentor's lot, Black Bean found a new place to call home with team 4646. Some of her favorite activities include exploring the ceiling of the workshop, playing with wires and other miscellaneous items, and eating conviently laid out food. Everything team 4646 does is now in her honor, and everything is also named after her.

Bean was helping the team wire the robot.

Christmas of 2024, Bean recieved a crocheted mouse toy from one of the mentors.
