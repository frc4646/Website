Team 4646 Wildcard Robotics is a FIRST Robotics Competition team based in Polk City, Iowa. They consist of students from North Polk High School, Ankeny Centennial High School, Ankeny High School, and Madrid High School. Wildcard started in 2021, taking over Team ASAP. The team meets about 4 times a week in Polk City during robotics season, while also providing times off-season for students to continue learning.
To join Wildcard, visit our join page or contact us.
