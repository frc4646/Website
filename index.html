Team 4646 Wildcard Robotics is a FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team based in Polk City, Iowa. We bring together students from North Polk, Ankeny Centennial, Ankeny, Madrid, and other nearby schools to design, build, and compete with high-level robots.

Founded in 2021 as a continuation of Team ASAP, Wildcard is built on a passion for engineering, teamwork, and innovation. During the robotics season, we meet about four times a week in Polk City, with additional opportunities in the off-season for students to keep learning, building, and improving their skills.

To join Wildcard, visit our join page or contact us.
